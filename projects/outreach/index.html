Roberto Carlos and the most beautiful goal in the world

Physics Time is a greek-language periodical published by the Postgrad Student Association of the Physics department of the University of Athens. It attempt to fill the gap between pop-sci articles and textbooks, giving the everybody from the interested high-schooler to the graduate who’s moved on to greener pastures something to chew on.

I contributed an article on the Magnus Effect, it lives here.

Worldline Drawing Tool

A charismatic professor once tried to explain the Lorentz transformation through the use of an imaginary program which depending on the strength of the boost would draw the transformation. I made it real, using pure JavaScript for easy deployment. Check it out!

LaTeX tutorial

I gave a colloquium on typesetting documents with TeX to the Free and Open Source Software (FOSS) Group in Physics Department of the University of Athens. Overleaf’s prevalence and improvement has made local TeX kind of obsolete, but I still think its worth knowing how to set it up.
